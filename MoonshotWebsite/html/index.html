How It Works

Photovoltaic Panels

Our panels made up of metal oxide films and photo-electrochemical cell, will capture and store the carbon dioxide by using the reaction from ultraviolet rays, infrared rays, and titanium dioxide composites.

Hybrid Sodium Carbon Dioxide

Once the carbon dioxide is stored, a carbon capture system called the Hybrid Sodium Carbon Dioxide System will do its part by producing hydrogen fuel that generates electricity.

Transformation

Once the electricity is produced, we will transfer it over to homes or structures through powerful conductors such as metal. Users can monitor energy being used through an application we will be creating.
